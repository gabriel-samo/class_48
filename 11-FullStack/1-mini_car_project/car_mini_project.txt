fullstack mini project
======================

backend (express+typescript) using local storage:
	routes:
		1. add car (get car info by license plate from data.gov.il)
		2. car sold (will not appear in car list)
		3. search car by manufacturer
		4. search car by price
		5. search car by model
		6. list all sold cars


frontend (react+typescript):
	using the backend with axios
	show all the option in backend.
